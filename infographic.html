How to be more

creative

1

Allows for new ideas and perspectives.

Stay receptive to new concepts, avoid immediate judgment, and be willing to explore unconventional solutions.


2

Reduces stress, improves focus, and fosters mental clarity.

Dedicate a few minutes daily to

meditation or mindfulness exercises, focusing on your breath and staying present.


3

Captures fleeting ideas and helps track creative thoughts.

Keep a journal or a digital note-taking app handy to jot down ideas, inspirations, and observations as they occur.


4

Refreshes the mind and prevents burnout, leading to better creativity.

Incorporate regular short breaks into your work schedule, and ensure you get sufficient rest and sleep.


5

Stimulates new ways of thinking and broadens your perspective.

Experiment with new hobbies, explore different cultures, learn new skills, or simply take a different route in your daily routine.


6

Combines diverse perspectives and skills, leading to innovative ideas.

Engage in group projects, join creative communities, or seek out brainstorming sessions with colleagues or friends.
